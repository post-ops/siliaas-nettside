# KPI Playbook - Weekly Operating Model

## Goal
Turn traffic into qualified conversations by continuously improving:
1) CTA rate
2) Form conversion
3) Scroll quality

---

## KPIs (track weekly)

### 1. CTA Rate
formula: cta_click / pageviews  
target: 3-8%

### 2. Form Conversion
formula: form_submit / form_start  
target: 40-70%

### 3. Scroll Quality
formula: scroll_75 / pageviews  
target: 30%+

---

## Weekly Routine (strict)

1. Wait until minimum 100-300 sessions
2. Pull last 7-14 days data
3. Evaluate KPIs
4. Choose ONE change only
5. Ship change
6. Wait next period
7. Repeat

---

## Decision Engine (use every time)

### A) CTA high, Form low
Problem: friction in form  
Actions:
- remove fields
- shorten copy
- add reassurance ("no commitment")
- clarify what happens next

---

### B) Scroll low
Problem: weak top of page  
Actions:
- stronger headline
- clearer value
- reduce fluff
- move proof higher

---

### C) Sticky > Hero
Problem: hero underperforming  
Actions:
- move sticky messaging into hero
- simplify hero choices
- strengthen primary CTA

---

### D) Micro CTA high
Problem: user not ready to contact  
Actions:
- add more specs / cases above fold
- surface technical proof earlier
- add downloadable / preview content

---

## Event Reference

- cta_click (location)
- form_start
- form_submit
- scroll_25 / scroll_50 / scroll_75
- email_click
- phone_click
- micro_cta_click (type)

---

## Guardrails

- Never change multiple things at once
- Do not optimize based on <100 sessions
- Prefer clarity over creativity
- Always prioritize reducing friction

---

## Success Signal

You are winning when:
- CTA rate increases or stays stable
- Form conversion increases
- More qualified conversations are generated

---

## Notes

This is a lead-generation system, not a design project.  
All changes must tie back to measurable improvement.

---

## KPI Change Checklist
- [ ] Which KPI are we improving?
- [ ] What scenario (A/B/C/D) are we addressing?
- [ ] What single change are we making?
- [ ] When will we evaluate results?
